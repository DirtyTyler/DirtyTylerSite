Double Rewards on all Blood Money Opportunities in Red Dead Online

Pick up Bonus Gold on Daily Challenges, Enjoy 2X RDO$ and XP for Competing in the Featured Series, and More

March 22nd, 2023 Dirtt Tyler

Earn double rewards for all Blood Money Opportunities in Red Dead Online, including 2X RDO$, Gold, and XP for participating in the Featured Series and completing Blood Money Contracts. You can also receive additional rewards for completing these opportunities and contracts.

From February 28, 2023, choose a life of crime and benefit from the bonuses. Complete 3 Blood Money Contracts per week to acquire the grey Valdez Vest. If you participate in a Persistent Posse and complete any Blood Money Opportunity, you'll receive a Gold Bar. Additionally, each week, determined criminals can earn the following rewards:

  • February 28 – March 6: Complete the Covington Emerald Blood Money Opportunity to receive gray Helsby Gloves
  • March 7 – 13: Complete select Blood Money Crimes (Stagecoach Stickup, A Lit Fuse, Wrongful Property, Back Pay) to receive a beige Gator Hat
  • March 14 – 20: Complete the Il Sovrano Blood Money Opportunity to receive a black pair of Plata Pants
  • March 21 – 27: Complete 10 Daily Challenges this week and receive a gray Caffyns Hat
  • March 28 – April 3: Complete the Ember of the East Blood Money Opportunity to receive the silver and brown version of Manstilla Poncho

All Rewards will be delivered within 72 hours of completion.

Get Bonus Capitale

Plus, you’re more likely to happen upon Capitale in chests and in the pockets of felled foes when taking on Blood Money Contracts through April 3.
