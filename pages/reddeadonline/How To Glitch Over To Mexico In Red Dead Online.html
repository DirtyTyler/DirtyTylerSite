Mexico has been hiding in Red Dead Redemption 2 all this time

Spotted by VG24/7, a dataminer on the GTA Forums has posted screenshots of models for familiar Mexico locations that they say have been dug out of the PS4 version. User VideoTech UK explains that the models they have found are for recognizable locations like Chuparosa that existed in the original Red Dead Redemption

It is crucial to understand, like VideoTech acknowledges, that the models (and textures) in question are of LOD quality, indicating that they are low-resolution models intended to be viewed from afar. In the event that these locations were genuinely intended to be explored within the game, higher-detail versions of the models should have been created. Additionally, VideoTech UK discovered these files in the original PC release of RDR2, rather than in a recent update. Therefore, it can be concluded that these models have been present in the game's files all along and do not indicate any upcoming changes.
